Facebook

Facebook is a California-based social networking site, formerly known as Facemash, that you can use to easily connect and communicate with people online . It was launched on February 4, 2004, by its creator, Mark Zuckerberg.

Instagram

Instagram is a popular American social media and photo/video sharing platform owned by Meta Platforms, where users can upload media, apply filters, and share it with followers through posts and temporary stories

Telegram

A telegram (or wire) was historically a message sent via electric telegraph, a system that allowed for rapid communication over long distances before modern telecommunications App
